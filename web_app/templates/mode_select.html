What the System Analyzes

These factors are evaluated internally during analysis.

Water

  • Rainfall patterns
  • Irrigation levels
  • Soil moisture conditions

Why it matters: Improper water levels are a common cause of plant stress and root damage.

Sunlight

  • Daily sunlight exposure
  • Crop-specific light requirements

Why it matters: Adequate sunlight supports photosynthesis and healthy growth.

Climate & Environment

  • Temperature
  • Humidity
  • Local climate type (e.g., Tropical Wet, Urban Heat Zone)

Why it matters: Climate affects watering needs, pest activity, and overall plant health.
